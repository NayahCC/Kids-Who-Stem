Hydrauliac Arm

In this area is where we have put the experiments we did over the span of this project. Pascal’s Principle in fluid mechanics states that for “a fluid at rest in a closed container, a pressure change in one part is transmitted without loss to every portion of the fluid and to the walls of the container.” This is the science you see behind Hydraulics! Because the liquid in the pipe resist compression, the pressure stays constant no matter what.Now the pressure is defined as the force acting per unit of area. About the Project:

Imagine being a girl interested in STEM, but not having the resources, or being told only boys can do "this". You would be devastated, disappointed because you couldn't delve deeper into what you might've been interested in. you gave up on your interest and continued in life. Maybe when you got older you may have revisited your interest, but not withthe same excitement as before. That's what our website is for; our website is a website for girls interested in STEM at a young age, so that they can learn and work on experiments with parental guideance. Not only will they be building projects that are fun, but projects of variety that cost little to do. Our website provides pictures of the resulting products so girls can see if they are interested in making the product and a little is added to the picture so they know a little about what they are making and why it has that affect.Below each picture is a link that will send them to a video about each picture that click on so they can follow along to a tutorial.
